ASSETS- The Finance Society

  • A.S.S.E.T.S is the Finance and Investment Society of Delhi Technological University (formerly Delhi College of Engineering).
  • A.S.S.E.T.S. stands for, Accounting Skills and Stock Exchange Training Society.
  • It was initially started an aim to nurture financially prudent students and start an investing culture.
  • It is the society that focuses on imparting basic understanding of ‘how to manage your money’.
  • Our vision is to create the next generation of smart and financially prudent individuals and we are on the path to spread financial literacy.
